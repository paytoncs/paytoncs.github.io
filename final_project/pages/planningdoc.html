Site Planning

Project Purpose

The purpose of thise site is to showcase my personal talents as a web designer and developer. The site is set up to lead people through pages which show examples of my work and also help them get to know me. It is designed to get them to a point where they are ready to contact and hire me for any type of work that they may need.

The Audience

Who is using the site?

  1. Business owners and managers
  2. Males and females ages 18-100
  3. Business status - not up to date with online business
  4. Not enough experience and time to design a site
  5. Busy
  6. At work on their desktop or laptop

Web Pages

  1. Home
    • This page will be the welcoming landing page that described the purpose of the website and helps the visitor know what expected of them.
  2. Logo Work
    • This page will be designed asa showcase of images and descriptions of all my logo work.
  3. Development Work
    • This page will be set up to show examples of my web site developement skills and explain why I'm a good choice for hiring.
  4. Get to Know Me
    • This page will be set up to connect with the audience by introducing myself and my family.
  5. My Resume
    • This page will be a simple resume, showing my talents and accomplishements.
  6. Hire Me
    • This page will be set up to explain how I can be contacted and hired and my rates.
