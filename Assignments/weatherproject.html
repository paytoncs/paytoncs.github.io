Site Purpose Statement

The purpose of this site is to deliver the weather in a manner that is easy to read. This site will contain the waeather of 3 different cities so it will need to be easy to navigate, finding city, date, and weather conditions.

section id=opp>

Audience Description

The audience for this site will be for almost all different ages over the age of about 12. Young and old are interested in the weather and for this reason it should be extremely easy to read and navigate for those that are not so web savvy. The audience will also be from the 3 different cities that we cover so we cannot favor one city over the other.

Develop an Audience Persona

PERSONA:

Average hardworking dad with a busy schedule and impatience with technology. Average age 25-60 This dad is impatient and doesnt have time to fiddle around with a complex website. Lives in the the city with his small family. He wants to know the weather for his drive to work and also for his family vacation the coming weekend.(Will also include females)

NEEDS AND EXPECTATIONS:

Needs to check the weather for his drives to and from work and his family plans on the weekends.

SITE USAGE:

Site will be used on a mobile device on the freeway on his way to work. Possibly may be used in the evenings on the computer to check tomorrows weather. The site will need to be simple for this average, busy, short tempered family man.
