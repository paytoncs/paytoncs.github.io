The media attribute specifies what media/device the linked document is optimized for.

The following are the linked documents or tags that can use the media attribute.


There are two parts to a media tag:

  • 1.Device

  • 2.Value

The device is self explanitory and defines which device the link is optimized for.

Examples: Projection, Print, Screen, TV etc.

The value gives you the ability to even further cutomize your optimization settings

Examples: Width, Heighth, Orientation etc.
